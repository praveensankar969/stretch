Small movements, often.

Stretch lives quietly in your system tray and invites you to unfold for one minute every so often. No alarms. No streaks-to-protect. Just a gentle reminder that you have a body.

How often?

Pick a cadence that won't break your focus. You can change it anytime.

Quiet hours

No reminders after hours or during Windows Focus Assist. This is on by default — you can turn it off later.

It will appear in the system tray — the small icons near your clock.
